# 【レポートH：ビジネスデザイン】 (2026-04-03)

## 1. エグゼクティブ・サマリー
The recent news highlights a growing trend where design is increasingly integrated into core business strategy, moving beyond mere aesthetics to drive innovation and revenue. The case of the capsule hotel leveraging sleep data for a "sleep tech business" demonstrates how understanding user behavior and data can lead to the creation of entirely new service offerings. Similarly, Morinaga's success with "Chocoball" by targeting "kidults" through design showcases the power of user-centric design and targeted marketing in expanding market share. The Fast Company article further emphasizes the evolving landscape of design roles, particularly in the context of AI, suggesting that designers need to adapt to more strategic and leadership-oriented positions to navigate the changing dynamics within product development teams. These examples underscore the importance of a holistic service design approach that considers not only the user experience but also the underlying business model, technological capabilities, and organizational structure.
